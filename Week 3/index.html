More About Me

My dream is simple: I want to create great work in a job that I love. I studied Finance undergrad and upon graduation I explored many options within the Finance industry, none of which excited me. I have seen people work countless hours in jobs that made them miserable. I knew what I didn’t want.

I was talking to my grandfather one day and he told me “If you don’t like what you are doing, then change it.” It is something so simple but had never crossed my mind. I had already spent years and money studying valuations and investments. After that advice, I started to look into other careers, ones that I was passionate about. After research and talking to people in the field, I discovered this career and and for the first time in awhile, I was excited about something. This is why I am here. My dream is to enjoy what I am doing. I hope to create work that I am proud of and passionate about.
